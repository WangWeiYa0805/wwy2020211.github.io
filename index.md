## 项目经历  projects

2020.01-2020.06

UAV fixed-point hovering based on computer vision

The YOLO algorithm is used to detect the hovering target point of
the drone, and the relative position of the drone to the hovering
point is calculated by using the camera ranging and 3D modeling
method. And plan the optimal path from the current position to
the hover point to control the dynamic stability of the drone hovering


2021.03-2021.06

Public opinion analysis based on faster multi-modal semantics

Design the public opinion analysis module to analyze the sentiment expressed in the collected news data (including video, audio
and text) . I try to combine convolution and attention in the design
for transformer model to solve the length of input problem and reduce the space complexity.At the same time, the model can also
dynamically train new data to update parameters.To reduce time
consume,I learn and try to use CUDA to accelerate the model. Based on the density feature, combined with the transformer-based
MulT and CNN modules, the MLP structure plan is embedded in
the work. This work was rejected for AAAI2022, and the plan is
to be as my master graduation project after improvement


2021.06-2021.08

Risk extraction in news

On the basis of news risk extraction, news risks are extracted
and classified based on the Bert model, including topic extraction, knowledge extraction and multi-risk classification. I mainly
study multi-language multi-risk classification, combined with the
ideas of tutor learning and bert for research


2021.12-2022.06

Semi-self-supervised large model with data compression

Design a pre-trained model to let fine-tuning of downstream tasks
can be adapted to visual tasks such as detection and classification.In training process,an effective data compression method
can reduce the time consume obviously.The pre-training method
that reduces the training time to 0.6-0.7 of the original and improves the accuracy of the model on detection task.


2020.01-2020.06 

基于计算机视觉的无人机定点悬停

这是我的本科项目。使用 YOLO 算法检测无人机的悬停目标点，以
及无人机与悬停的相对位置点是通过使用相机测距和 3D 建模方法
计算的。并规划从当前位置到悬停点的最优路径，控制无人机悬停
的动态稳定性


2021.03-2021.06

Public opinion analysis based on faster multi-modal semantics

设计舆情分析模块，对收集到的新闻数据（包括视频、音频和文本）
中表达的情绪进行分析。在 transformer 模型的设计中尝试将卷积
和 attention 结合起来，解决输入长度问题，降低空间复杂度。同时，
模型还可以动态训练新数据来更新参数。为了减少时间消耗，我学
习了并尝试使用 CUDA 来加速模型。在算法上，基于密度特征，结
合基于 transformer 的 MulT 和 CNN 模块，将 MLP 结构方案嵌
入到工作中。本作品被 AAAI2022 拒，计划改进后作为我的硕士毕
业课题


2021.06-2021.08

Risk extraction in news

在新闻风险抽取的基础上，基于 Bert 模型对新闻风险进行抽取和
分类，包括主题抽取、知识抽取和多风险分类。我主要研究多语言
多风险分类，结合导师学习和 bert 的思路进行研究


2021.12-2022.06

结合数据压缩方法的半自监督大模型研发

可以很好地表征输入数据，使下游任务的微调适应检测、分类等视
觉任务. 在训练过程中，有效的数据压缩方法可以明显减少时间消
耗。最后成功研究出来一种训练过程中基于梯度的样本选取的压缩
算法，实现训练时间压缩到原本百分之六十至七十同时改进的预训
练方法可以使模型在检测任务在场景数据集上准确率达到良好效果
